Reading books has been a go-to pastime for me throughout my life, and teachers and friends from my school - especially the librarians, have been instrumental in developing my reading habits. In terms of the books I like, I very rarely tend to tread new waters, and try to stick to authors and character I know and love already. As a consequence, I have ended up reading from just a few authors over the course of school life. However, the complexity of the books I have read has increased gradually over time. In this site, I have mentioned five books each from the three authors I have read of predominantly. This is a compilation of blurbs, reviews, and summaries from the internet.
